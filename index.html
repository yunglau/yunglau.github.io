Elaine Lau

Affiliations Scale AI, McGill University, Mila

I am a Machine Learning Research Engineer at Scale AI, building reasoning datasets, training pipelines, and safety evaluation frameworks for LLM-based browser agents. I partner with customers and research groups to deliver applied ML systems.

Previously, I was a researcher at Valence Labs – Recursion, developing QGFN by combining GFlowNets with action-value guidance for diverse solution discovery in drug discovery, and a graduate researcher at McGill University and Mila advised by Doina Precup and Emmanuel Bengio.
